# JPMorgan Chase – Quantitative Research Virtual Experience (Forage)

Solutions for all 4 tasks from the JPMorgan Chase Quantitative Research 
job simulation on Forage.

## Tasks

**Task 1 – Natural Gas Price Estimator**  
Extrapolates monthly natural gas prices using linear regression with 
seasonal Fourier terms. Takes any date and returns a price estimate.

**Task 2 – Commodity Storage Contract Pricer**  
Prices a natural gas storage contract by calculating purchase cost, 
revenue, and storage fees across injection and withdrawal dates.

**Task 3 – Credit Risk Model**  
Logistic regression model trained on 10,000 loan records to predict 
probability of default. Outputs expected loss using PD × LGD × EAD.

**Task 4 – FICO Score Bucketing**  
Dynamic programming approach to find optimal FICO score bucket boundaries 
that maximize log-likelihood. Maps any FICO score to a credit rating.

## Tools Used
Python, pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, matplotlib, scipy
